What is GreenTrip Guru?

GreenTrip Guru is a web application that helps you plan your vacation in a sustainable way. It provides you with a list of eco-friendly hotels, restaurants, and activities in the city of your choice while also considering your priorities and reviews of previous vacations. You can also calculate the carbon footprint of your trip and find out how to offset it.
